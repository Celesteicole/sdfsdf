What We Do

Focused services for real projects and real budgets.

Three offerings cover most client needs; each can expand to fit complex sites or regulatory reviews.

A

Custom Homes & Major Renovations

We design new residences and transformative additions with envelope-first thinking: orientation, insulation, and mechanical systems that reduce bills without sacrificing comfort. Our documents guide competitive bidding and efficient construction.

B

Adaptive Reuse & Small Commercial

How We Work

A three-stage path from first sketch to keys in hand.

01 • Mapping

Listen, test, decide

We meet on site, take quick measurements, review surveys, and gather your wishlist. In two weeks we return with two design directions and an outline budget so you can choose a path with confidence.

02 • Detailing

Drawings that get built

Selected concept becomes a permit set: plans, elevations, wall sections, window/door schedules, and specifications. We coordinate structural and MEP engineers, then help you solicit comparable bids.

03 • Building

Guardrails during construction

We visit at milestones—foundation, framing, insulation, finishes—answer RFIs, review pay apps, and keep the project aligned with the drawings and your budget. You move in with a tidy punch list and an as-built set.
